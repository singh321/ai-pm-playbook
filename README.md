# AI PM Playbook

A practical collection of frameworks, operating models, and decision tools for building AI-native products.

This repository reflects how I think about AI product management in practice — across product strategy, GenAI workflows, agentic systems, RAG products, evaluation, rollout, monetization, and scale.

It is designed for:
- AI Product Managers
- Product Leaders
- Founders
- PM candidates preparing for AI product roles
- Teams building AI-native products

---

## Why this repo exists

Most AI PM content is either too generic or too technical.

This repo focuses on the middle layer that matters most in real product work:

- turning AI capability into customer value
- deciding what should be automated vs assisted
- measuring AI product quality
- building trust in AI workflows
- sequencing MVP to scale
- connecting AI systems with business outcomes

The thinking here is shaped by my experience across AI, SaaS, fintech, agritech, platform products, and 0→1 to scale journeys.

## How I use these frameworks

These frameworks are not academic.

They are designed to help answer real product questions such as:
- Should this AI feature be recommendation-only or fully automated?
- What metrics matter beyond engagement?
- How do we decide MVP scope for an AI workflow?
- How do we build trust for LLM-powered experiences?
- When is model quality good enough to launch?
- How do we prioritize AI features with limited engineering capacity?
